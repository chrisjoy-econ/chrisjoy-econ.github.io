CHRISTOPHER J. JOY

About

I'm a researcher interested in developing economies with a particular focus on Southeast Asia's rapid growth and rise over the last 20 years. My work is interdisciplinary, pulling from such diverse topics as female empowerment, poverty reduction, and state-society relations. I hold an MSc in Development Economics and an MA in Southeast Asian Studies from City, University of London and SOAS, Universty of of London respectively. I speak Vietamese, Indonesian and Thai.

I'm currently open to consultant and freelance opportunities. chrisjoy at sent.com
